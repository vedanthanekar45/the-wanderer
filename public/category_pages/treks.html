Get your hiking gears on!

Popular treks in and around Nashik:

B R A H M A G I R I

Situated near the town of Trimbak, approximately 36 kilometers from Nashik City, the Brahmagiri Hill offers scenic beauty and historical significance, making it a favored destination for trekkers and pilgrims alike.

P A N D A V L E N I

Tucked away in the captivating vistas of Nashik concealed treasure harmoniously combines adventure and spirituality- Pandavleni. Legend has it that these caves were named after the Pandavas, who sought solace within the rocky confines during their exile.

R A M S H E J

The meaning of Ramshej is Lord Ram's bedstead. It is believed that Lord Rama had made this place as his residence for some time during their exile.
